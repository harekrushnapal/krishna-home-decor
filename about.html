Our Story

Krishna Home Decor, located in Jaleswar, Odisha, is your one-stop destination for home furnishing needs. Whether you're looking to revamp your space or add cozy touches to your home, we offer a curated range of products including cushion covers, curtains, bedsheets, carpets, wallpapers, and high-quality cleaning tools.

With a blend of traditional values and modern aesthetics, we bring to you collections from top brands like Gala, Milton, Welspun, Nayasa, Spaces, Universe Mattress, and Fast Clean. Our store is a proud local favorite and a trusted name in both retail and wholesale markets.
